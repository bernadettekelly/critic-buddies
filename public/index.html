Rear Window

Nora Kelly
Dec 13th 17



Rear Window is my favorite Hitchcock film. It's a prime example of his 50s style and builds suspense within a very limted setting. Jimmy Stewart is the ideal every man hero, and Grace Kelly is the classic Hitchcock herione - a cool, beautiful blonde on the outside, and fiery on the inside.
